# Pearl Brand Reference Screenshots

Vetted visual reference assets for the Pearl brand synthesis Notion board.

Capture rule: each site screenshot was taken after visible cookie or consent prompts were accepted, declined, or closed. For app products and browser-blocked product pages, official brand/App Store imagery is included where it is more representative than a broken or obstructed capture.

Scope source: `16 Final Brand Synthesis + Decision Guide`, with removed sources excluded. Mr & Mrs Smith, Small Luxury Hotels, The Row, Travel + Leisure, Aura, NetJets, VistaJet, TCS World Travel, Four Seasons Private Jet, Scott Dunn, Black Tomato, and San Vicente Bungalows are intentionally out of scope.

Fallback notes:
- Loro Piana and a few luxury product pages blocked automated browser capture at the protocol layer. For those, the board uses official source imagery from their public pages instead of pretending they are normal browser screenshots.
- Negative/tension references are included only as boundary examples.
